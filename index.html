This project is for group 17 members which include Kisekka Andrew | Nanteza Jeanne Jacobet | Lutaaya Patrick | Akantorana Moses | Natumanya Naboth. This project was our recess term task whereby it aims at improving and simplifying life in the field of travelling both for the case of travellers or you can call them customers in this context, bus drivers or employees in this context and the bus owners or the employers.

Problem

Travelling to and from villages is stressful for bus travellers and bus company staff especially during festive seasons and COVID19 lockdowns. This leads to a number of problems i.e. missing of transport means, delays and at times expensive.

Solution

Given all these underlying problems, we the members of the group 17 have decided to come up with the YoTicket application. This will enable to streamline the booking systems at bus stations.

Project objectives

  • Build a reliable bus ticket booking mobile application.
  • Streamline the booking systems at bus stations.

Project Requirements

  • Hosting space.
  • Internet connection.
  • Mobile operating system (Android, iOS).

Target Users

  • Travellers: These will book tickets on buses with ease to travel to their destinations.

  • Managers: View the statistics and organized view of operations at the bus company.

  • Drivers & Conductors : View the numbers of customers and let people in the bus using the app plus confirm people have paid for the journey.

Project Requirements

  • Implement more payment options so that users have more choices of payment while paying for tickets.
  • Integrate USSD technology into the platform so that people without internet can be able to book tickets.
  • Introduce an option for users to be able to print the tickets they have bought.

Conclusion

In conclusion, we strongly believe that application will help streamline the bus booking process reducing congestions around these places, reducing the time it takes to get a ticket for the bus, and also travelling during busy times like festive seasons is made easier.
